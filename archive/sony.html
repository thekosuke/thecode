Enhancing Creativity in Video Production with AI Co-Creation

Sony

|

UX/UI Design

Prototyping

|

2024

This is an ongoing collaboration with Sony on an innovative project to develop a production design tool that empowers video creators to co-create with AI. This initiative aims to integrate Sony's advanced digital cameras with their existing Creator's Cloud platform.

My primary role involves implementing the strategy and concepts derived from comprehensive design research, which included conversations with video creators from various regions, expertise levels, and production styles.
