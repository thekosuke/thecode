Background

Towards the end of my undergraduate program, I had the opportunity to work as an intern at two design agencies—R/GA Tokyo and Green Stone. Learning from seasoned designers and working in cross-functional teams prepared me for what’s to come after my student life.

Getting exposure to how design is treated in a business context and seeing a designer’s role in relation to the clients helped me ponder the career path I want to take moving forward.

R/GA Tokyo

In Summer 2019, I joined R/GA Tokyo as an Experience Design Intern for 13 weeks. At the time, the Tokyo office consisted of roughly 35-40 people with each team member juggling multiple client work. The fast-paced environment was very stimulating and it was the closest thing to a flat organization that I have experienced. I was immediately thrown into the fire to contribute rather than to sit and watch — do and learn is my style after all.

During my time there, I supported the team on various client projects while observing how each person adapts to different projects and roles. My work included UX research, conceptualizing a pop-up retail experience, facilitating ideation workshops, designing the user experience for Google's B2B tools, and prototyping for a client pitch. Clients include Shiseido, Google APAC, Clé de Peau Beauté, and other undisclosed clients.

Green Stone

Green Stone is a fully remote digital experience agency that adopted the remote model before it was cool. At Green Stone, I received mentorship from multiple Senior Experience Designers with work that tied to my growth goals. This included a website renewal for Elevations Credit Union, visualizing concepts for an early-stage startup, and designing system diagrams for a client pitch.

As part of my internship, I was given the opportunity to visually articulate various concepts for Giide, an early-stage startup that aims to enable businesses to easily create and publish interactive audio content.

I worked directly with the founders to define the user experience of their content creation platform by making proposals through mid to high-fidelity mockups. My work also consisted of visualizing the company's product ecosystem and target market for investment opportunities. This helped the team at Giide have a comprehensive view of their business and product to communicate holistically.
