Hi, my name is Kosuke Futsukaichi.

I'm a designer living and working in Tokyo as a member of Takram, a global design innovation studio. My focus is digital product and brand design, but I engage in every stage of the process—from research to execution.

Simply put, I solve user experience and user interface problems for apps, software, digital tools, and websites. My strength is translating abstract concepts into tangible and intuitive UI, while placing a strong emphasis on visual polish.

Culturally, I'm a product of the Kantō region of Japan, Toronto, Arizona, and Vancouver. Over time, these diverse environments have shaped a hybrid of Japanese sensibility and Hip-Hop audacity.

I aspire to spend my life working on projects that make use of my creativity and, hopefully, inspire others along the way.

Please reach out for project inquiries or simply to connect.
