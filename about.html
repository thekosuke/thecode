Hi, my name is Kosuke Futsukaichi.

I'm a designer living and working in Tokyo as a member of Takram, a global design innovation studio. My primary focus is in digital product and brand design.

I specialize in articulating a brand’s essence or product concepts through strategic thinking and visual communication. My work revolves around transforming abstract concepts into tangible, intuitive, and polished solutions.

To me, design is a holistic practice and culture—more than just a service to businesses, it’s a lens through which I see and interpret the world.

Culturally, I'm a product of the Kantō region of Japan, Toronto, Arizona, and Vancouver. Over time, these diverse environments have produced a hybrid of Japanese sensibility and Hip-Hop audacity.

I aspire to spend my life doing things that utilize my creativity and, hopefully, inspire others along the way.

Please feel free to reach out for project inquiries or simply to connect.
