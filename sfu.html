Background

My foundation in design comes from the School of Interactive Arts & Technology program at Simon Fraser University. I was fortunate to be exposed to graphic, brand, type, product, spatial, and digital design.

I'm now primarily in the field of digital product, fascinated by the notion of creating something from nothing. I aim to design for practical experiences that solve relevant problems in new and exciting ways.

italiaDesign

In 2018, I was one of twelve senior students selected to participate in italiaDesign, an 8-month undergraduate field school program directed by Russell Taylor. The team interviewed emerging and established designers in Italy to learn how the discipline of design is practiced in a foreign context. We encapsulated these interviews in a web experience to contribute to the legacy of italiaDesign field school.

Touchpoint 2019

Touchpoint Interaction Design Conference is a part of Simon Fraser University and the School of Interactive Art and Technology for the Interaction Design Research Centre. Touchpoint is ran annually with each year having its own visual identity. I volunteered to design and build the website for Touchpoint 2019.
