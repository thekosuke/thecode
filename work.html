About

Hi, I'm Kosuke — a Japanese designer now living in Vancouver, Canada. I'm currently a full-time designer at Trustworthy, The Family Operating System® to keep your family organized and prepared in one place.

Growing up, I was fascinated by new gadgets and how it gave people superpowers. This curiosity led me to pursue a degree in design at Simon Fraser University. It was here that I learned not only about the design process but also developed a point of view on my design practice.

Culturally, I'm a product of the Kantō region of Japan, Toronto, Arizona, and now Vancouver. Over time, these environments produced a hybrid of Japanese sensibility and Hip-Hop audacity. I enjoy making with people, listening to people, and observing the world with an open mind.

I now design full-time and aspire to become a designer with a capital D.
