italiaDesign

In 2018 I participated in italiaDesign, an 8-month field school program where the team traveled to Italy and studied the countryâ€™s rich design history and culture. We experienced different cities and interviewed emerging and established Italian designers to learn their unique design practices. For these designers, Design meant something more than a title. It was more of an attitude, or a way of seeing the world in which they embrace everyday.

After our trip, we distilled our learnings into a series of videos capsuled inside a web experience. I contributed to the entirety of the project and helped design and build the 11th edition of the italiaDesign website.


Visit website: 2018.sfuitaliadesign.com
