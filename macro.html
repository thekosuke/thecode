Summary

In April 2020, I joined Macro as their first employee. As the founding designer at a B2B SaaS startup, I generated design solutions that are practical, scalable, and intentional.

Macro is a process management tool for teams to design and deploy custom operational processes without writing code. The challenge was to enable teams to do so in a simple, personal, and comprehensive way.

I worked directly with the co-founders to develop the product from zero to one. I conducted user interviews, identified problems, explored countless iterations, and collaborated with engineers to turn my designs into a useable app.
