Background

Macro is a tool for operational process design. Our mission is to help companies better create, communicate, and execute processes with the aim to build a more humane way of performing repetitive work. We are reimagining checklists as the means to facilitate processes involving people and SaaS applications.

Our fundamental idea is that operational design is secretly a form of programming. That mode of programming deserves a tool specially built for the needs of modern Ops teams.

Designing Macro

I'm currently working with the founders to build the foundation of the Macro platform. As the founding designer, I execute on system-level design to shape the product and define the brand through a design lens.
