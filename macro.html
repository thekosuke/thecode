Background

Macro is a tool for people to design and deploy custom operational processes without writing code. Processes are something every company has but they are very unique to each company. Yet there isn't a tool right now that enable teams to represent their unique processes in a simple and comprehensive way. Macro reimagines checklists as the means to create, assign, and track processes involving people and software. Macro aspires to be the single tool that teams use to design and deploy their processes.

I'm currently designing the foundation of Macro with some fun people. As the sole designer, I generate practical design solutions to help shape the product and brand. We want to build a great product that people love.
