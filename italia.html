Summary

italiaDesign is an 8-month field school program offered by Simon Fraser University, where students research and experience Italy’s rich design culture. italiaDesign field school is run bi-annually with a team of 12 students directed by a professor.

After our trip to Italy, we produced a series of short films and made a website to present them. The intention behind the website was to document our experience and share our learnings with the community.

In order to build the website, I proposed a framework and direction that aligned with the content and our learnings. I lead the entire process—from conception to delivery—while contributing to storytelling, user experience, visual design, and web development.
