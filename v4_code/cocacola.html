XD Intern @ R/GA TYO

In Summer 2019, I joined R/GA Tokyo as an Experience Design Intern for 13 weeks. Throughout the internship, I worked on various client projects to support the team by conducting research, facilitating ideation workshops, crafting UX/UI for Google's B2B platforms, and prototyping for a client pitch.

During my stay, I had the opportunity to learn from a collective of genuinie individuals who deeply cared about the team, the culture, and their own craft.

In addition to client work, I also started on a self-directed client pitch during the second half of my internship. I was given a list of clients down the pipeline and was asked to create a brief that could potentially be pitched to the client. With this, I developed a pitch for Coca-Cola; reimagining the vending machine experience to help Coca-Cola be part of the solution to plastic pollution.
