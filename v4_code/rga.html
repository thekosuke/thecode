R/GA TYO

XD Intern

In Summer 2019, I joined R/GA Tokyo as an Experience Design Intern for 13 weeks. During my stay, I had the opportunity to learn from a collective of unique individuals who deeply cared about the team, the culture, and their own craft.

I supported the team on various client projects by conducting research, facilitating ideation workshops, crafting UX/UI for Google's B2B platforms, and prototyping for a client pitch. Clients include Shiseido, Google, Merck, Clé de Peau Beauté, and Mori Building. Although I am unable to share my work publicly, I'd be more than happy to talk about it in-person.

In addition to client work, I also worked on a self-directed pitch for Coca-Cola: reimagining the vending machine experience to help the company be part of the solution to plastic pollution.

Organic Relationships

One of the most important takeaways from my time at R/GA was a new understanding of how relationships could come in different forms.
