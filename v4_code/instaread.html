Instaread

Contract Designer

From August to December 2018, I was a contract product designer at Instaread, helping the company improve their product remotely. During my contract, I worked on various parts of their digital offerings as well as creating their App Preview and other promotional materials.

Being the only designer on the team, I was also responsible for making suggestions to improve their style guide and providing art direction for the illustrations.

Art Direction

I improved the Onboarding screens on their iOS app by providing art direction for the illustrations and designing the UI.

Since the color scheme of their digital products lacked unity, I suggested the company to use a monochromatic color scheme and let the illustrations act as the colors. Making use of the illustrations in the Onboarding screens was an incremental improvement to shift towards the new art direction.
