Background

In Summer 2019, I joined R/GA Tokyo as an Experience Design Intern for 13 weeks. I had the opportunity to work in multidisciplinary teams and learn how each person serves the purpose of the best result. Working in Tokyo was something I was very excited to experience as well. Special appreciation goes to the people at R/GA Tokyo. I had an invaluable experience.

During my time there, I supported the team on various client projects by conducting research, conceptualizing a pop-up retail experience, facilitating ideation workshops, designing the user experience for Google's B2B platforms, and prototyping for a client pitch. Clients include Shiseido, Google APAC, Clé de Peau Beauté, and other undisclosed clients. Although I am unable to share my work publicly, I'd be happy to share it in-person.
