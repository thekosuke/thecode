Background

After my experience at Macro, I sought to work at a company with a consumer-facing product where I could collaborate in cross-functional teams. My main priorities at this point were: the type of projects I will be working on, the type of problems I will be thinking about, and who I will be learning from.

This lead me to join Seven.me—a healthcare startup that aims to help people lead healthy and happy lives through behavioral health interventions. As employee #30 and designer #3, the team gave me the opportunity to spearhead the delivery side of design while refining my design practice with the help from other designers.
