Summary

Seven is a health-focused startup building a system of products and services that make health mastery accessible to all through behavioral health coaching. As their third designer, I was given the opportunity to lead the delivery team by owning the end-to-end experience of multiple projects with cross-functional stakeholders.

My main responsibilities were to revamp the client-facing mobile app while improving the internal web platform and the company website, as well as expressing the brand through marketing materials.
