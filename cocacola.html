Coca-Cola

Overview

During my time at R/GA Tokyo, I was given the opportunity to apply my learnings through a self-directed brief/pitch for Coca-Cola, which involved working with cultural frictions through the lens of experience design.

The project reimagines the vending machine experience to help Coca-Cola be part of the solution to plastic pollution. I presented the project to the team on my final week as part of my internship program.

Thank you for going through all those slides. This project best reflects my approach in experience design as well as the type of work I'm most interested in.

I'd love to go through the project in-depth and hear your thoughts on it.
